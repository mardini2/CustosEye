About CustosEye

CustosEye is a local-first security monitoring tool that gives you visibility into what's happening on your system. It watches your processes, monitors file integrity, and tracks network activity—all without sending data anywhere.

The Problem

Most security tools either require cloud subscriptions, leak telemetry to unknown servers, or are too complex for everyday use. You shouldn't need to trust a third party just to understand what's running on your own machine.

The Solution

CustosEye runs entirely on your machine. No cloud, no telemetry, no mystery. It uses a local trust engine to evaluate process behavior, monitors file changes via hash checks, and gives you a simple web dashboard to see everything at a glance.

Why It Exists

Built out of frustration with existing tools that were either too heavy, too intrusive, or too opaque. CustosEye is designed to be portable, private, and transparent—just download and run. Your data stays on your machine, and you control what gets monitored.
